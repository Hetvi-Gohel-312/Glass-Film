Advantages of using Glass Film over Etched Glass

Why our glass film solutions are superior to traditional etched glass

Cost-Effective

The glass film from Artful Home offers the look of an etched glass window but at a cheaper cost than etched glass.

Easy Installation

The glass film is easier to install than etched glass and requires less time to install to your design.

Enhanced Safety

The glass film holds glass together to lessen the likelihood of flying shards during natural and man-made disasters. This gives more safety in the workplace from injury and property damage.

Security

It becomes difficult for thefts and make forced entry with our safety films.

UV Protection

The glass film from Artful home screens up to 98% of damaging ultraviolet radiation and decreases interior fading. The glass film also cuts glare.

Scratch Resistant

The glass film comes with scratch proof coating for extended durability.

Easy to Update

It's always easy to change. When the design trend changes, it's easy to remove and change the glass film without any damage to the glass.

Wide Application

Window films are widely used in Office, IT Companies, Restaurant, hotels and resorts, retail showrooms, Jewellery Shops, Schools, Colleges, Hospitals, Residence.
